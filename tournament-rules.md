# APPENDIX B - MATCH CONFIGURATIONS

## Stage 2 - Knockouts

- Game mode: Multi-life Knockout
- Opponent ghosts: not enforced
- Number of starting lives: 3
- Number of maximum lives: 5
- Golden Round: enabled until 4 remaining players
- Loss-of-life thresholds:

| Remaining players in the match | # of players losing a life each round |
|---|---:|
| 19-25 | 5 |
| 13-18 | 4 |
| 9-12 | 3 |
| 5-8 | 2 |
| 1-4 | 1 |

- Number of tracks played: 6
- Rounds per track: 4
- Finish timeout: 15 seconds
- Track order: randomized
- Warm-up: 1 round
- Respawn: allowed

## Stage 2 - Dutch Qualification Match

- Game mode: Cup
- Match point limit: 140
- Point distribution: 10, 6, 4, 3
- Rounds per track: 4
- Finish timeout: 15 seconds
- Warm-up: 1 round
- Opponents: not enforced
- Track order: determined by player pick phase
- Respawn: allowed
